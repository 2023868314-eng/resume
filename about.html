About Me

My name is Nor'Aina Natasya Binti Mohammad Asri. I was born and raised in Seri Kembangan, Selangor, a place that shaped who I am today. As the youngest of five siblings, I grew up surrounded by love, laughter, and strong family values. I am blessed with two adorable and delightfully playful nieces who never fail to brighten my days. Above all, I am grateful for my parents, whose endless love and care bind our family together. I carry a firm spirit, yet I remain warm and approachable to those around me. An extrovert by nature and an independent woman at heart, I embrace challenges with confidence,especially when standing before an audience.
