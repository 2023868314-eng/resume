Experience

MAHA 2024

I volunteered at the MAHA 2024 exhibition for 12 consecutive days without any days off. We worked for 18 hours a day, from 6:00 a.m. to 12:00 midnight. It was a very interesting and valuable experience, as I had the opportunity to meet many visitors and get to know other fellow volunteers. I also gained a lot of knowledge from everyone I met there. I truly wish to participate again as a MAHA volunteer in the upcoming MAHA 2026.

Farm Freash @ UPM

Worked part-time as a facilitator for kindergarten students. Guided them around the Farm Fresh area, where they learned basic knowledge about how cows are cared for to produce milk, how milk is pumped from cows, and were introduced to composting. This experience helped improve my communication skills, especially when dealing with young children who can be quite challenging to handle..

Earthopia

I worked part-time in an office as a facilitator for Chinese schools. I taught students how to make soap and introduced them to basic robotics. I was selected for this role because I am fluent in Mandarin, which made it easier for the students to communicate and understand the lessons.

Deedapit Bakery & Cafe

I helped my sister at her bakery by cooking and preparing various types of desserts ordered by customers. Sometimes, I also assisted her during events when she provided services for vendors. It was quite enjoyable because I got to spend time with my sister, and it was fun being able to enjoy free desserts as well.
